Explore the health conditions our platform can assist in identifying

Diabetes

A metabolic disease that occurs when your blood glucose is too high, preventing your body from effectively utilizing energy.

Key Symptoms
  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst
  • Extreme fatigue
  • Blurry vision
Check for Diabetes

Breast Cancer

A devastating form of cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts, heavily prevalent and most frequently diagnosed in women.

Key Symptoms
  • Lump or thickening
  • Change in size or shape
  • Skin dimpling
  • Redness or pitting
Check for Cancer

Kidney Disease

The gradual loss of organ function, preventing the effective filtration of metabolic wastes and excess fluids from your blood.

Key Symptoms
  • Nausea & vomiting
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Muscle twitches
  • Cramps & swelling
Check for Kidney Disease

Heart Disease

A variety of conditions affecting the heart muscle, valves, or rhythm, leading to cardiovascular complications.

Key Symptoms
  • Chest discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in neck/jaw
  • Numbness in limbs
Check for Heart Disease

Liver Disease

A range of conditions that damage the liver and prevent it from functioning properly, often resulting in jaundice or abdominal swelling.

Key Symptoms
  • Jaundice (yellow skin)
  • Abdominal swelling
  • Dark urine color
  • Chronic fatigue
Check for Liver Disease

Malaria

A serious mosquito-borne infectious disease spread by parasites. Symptoms begin 10 to 15 days after being bitten by an infected mosquito.

Key Symptoms
  • High Fever
  • Shaking Chills
  • Severe Headache
  • Nausea and vomiting
Check for Malaria

Pneumonia

An infection inflating the air sacs in one or both lungs, which may fill with fluid or pus, producing a severe cough.

Key Symptoms
  • Cough with mucus
  • Fever & sweating
  • Shortness of breath
  • Rapid breathing
Check for Pneumonia
